Smarter Bookmarklet

How to Install:

  1. Drag the bookmarklet below to your browser's bookmarks bar
  2. Or right-click the bookmarklet and select "Bookmark this link"

How to Use:

  1. Click the bookmarklet on any webpage to open the Smarter panel
  2. Drag the panel by its header to move it around
  3. Resize the panel using the handle in the bottom-right corner
  4. Click the × button to close the panel
